The Grumpy Peer Reviewer

Most AI summarizes. V.E.R.A. interrogates. It asks the questions you wish you had time to ask.

Call It Out

V.E.R.A. classifies content brutally. Is it an "Empirical Study" or just a "Sales Pitch wearing a lab coat"? It distinguishes between Experience Reports, Exploratory Analysis, and pure Marketing Fluff.

Methodology Check

It hunts for the basics: Where did this data come from? How was it collected? What's the sample size? Could anyone reproduce this? If the answer is "no," V.E.R.A. says so.

Evidence Grading

From Grade A (Strong Evidence) down to Grade F (Unsubstantiated). It evaluates data transparency, technical depth, reproducibility, and independent validation.
